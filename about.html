About Rhys

My Story

Passion for the natural world

I'm Rhys — a nature and wildlife photographer with over 4 years of experience exploring the planet's most remote and breathtaking places. From African savannas to Arctic tundras, my lens has followed the wild wherever it leads.

I photograph everything — animals, landscapes, macro details, street life, forests, oceans, and skies. My philosophy is simple: every corner of this world deserves to be seen and celebrated.

I've traveled to 4+ countries, slept under the stars in Patagonia, waited days for a single shot in the Serengeti, and found magic in the smallest details right in my own backyard.
